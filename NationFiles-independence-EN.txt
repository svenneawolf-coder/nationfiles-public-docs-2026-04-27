Legal TL;DR & Disclaimer
	NationFiles is a privately funded, fully independent intelligence project of Neawolf Media Group. The NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI) and all derived geopolitical metrics are computed entirely automatically by the Naciro Engine using openly documented mathematical formulas (Layers 1–3). There are no government grants, no political influence, no paid rankings, and no manual score manipulation. Categories such as EXCELLENCE or DISASTER are purely technical classifications of data volatility, not political judgments. Forecasts are statistical extrapolations of data trends and do not constitute recommendations for action.
	The content, scores, and forecasts on NationFiles are for analytical and informational purposes only. Despite the highest technical care, NationFiles assumes no liability for direct or indirect damages or for investment, travel, or security decisions made on the basis of the information provided. For legally binding or life-saving operational decisions, the competent public authorities must be consulted.



________________________________________________________________________________
The 6 core principles



________________________________________________________________________________
100 % independent
	NationFiles operates without influence from government bodies, political parties, companies, or lobby groups. No external advertising is displayed; there are no paid placements or sponsorships that could influence data collection.



________________________________________________________________________________
Algorithmic equal treatment
	Each of the 190+ countries covered is assessed using the same, openly documented rules (Layers 1–3). There are no geopolitical exceptions, buffer zones, manual overrides, or "premium countries."



________________________________________________________________________________
No data manipulation
	All scores, trends, and forecasts from the Naciro Engine are produced fully automatically. Manual changes to scores by administrators are technically excluded. Corrections for faulty raw data are made only through reproducible recalculations.



________________________________________________________________________________
Transparent methodology (open formulas)
	Formulas, weightings, trend calculations (e.g. WLS), and volatility clamping are publicly documented. Every change to the mathematical model is versioned and communicated in the changelog.



________________________________________________________________________________
Neutral analytics & language
	Categories such as SOLID or UNSTABLE are purely technical classifications of system stability. NationFiles does not make political judgments, does not forecast election results, and does not give security-policy recommendations.



________________________________________________________________________________
Open data sources
	Only publicly accessible, machine-readable macro, event, and trend data are processed (see /legal/sources/). Secret or politically filtered government data without verification options are not included in the assessment.



________________________________________________________________________________
I. Political and institutional independence



________________________________________________________________________________
Principle
	NationFiles is a project of Neawolf Media Group and operates solely on the principle of radical objectivity.



________________________________________________________________________________
Funding
	The project is fully self-funded (bootstrapped) and supported by disclosed license sales (API access). Government grants, party-political funding, and donations from interest groups are strictly declined.



________________________________________________________________________________
Conflicts of interest
	Employees, developers, and advisors of Neawolf Media Group are contractually required to disclose potential financial or political conflicts of interest. Persons with direct access to the Naciro Engine codebase may not be involved in geopolitical decision-making processes.



________________________________________________________________________________
II. Algorithmic pipelines & equal treatment



________________________________________________________________________________
Unified pipeline
	The NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI) is computed via a global, unified pipeline.



________________________________________________________________________________
No discrimination
	Sanctioned states, non-UN members, or crisis regions go through the same scripts as G7 nations. There are no blocks on countries based on political directives.



________________________________________________________________________________
Automation
	The process from data retrieval (scraping/APIs) through normalization to score assignment runs without human intervention. Systems run uniformly worldwide and are monitored by automated plausibility checks.



________________________________________________________________________________
III. Methodology, reproducibility and versioning



________________________________________________________________________________
Layer model
	The assessment is composed of Layer 1 (economic and hard baseline data), Layer 2 (daily event aggregation), and Layer 3 (structural country index/rule of law). Formulas and weighting parameters are disclosed in the methodology whitepaper.



________________________________________________________________________________
Versioning (changelog)
	Every change to weighting or bug fix in the code receives a version number (e.g. Naciro Engine v1.2.4), a change date, and documented rationale. Historical scores remain mathematically reproducible through archiving of older versions.



________________________________________________________________________________
IV. Data sources, processing & quality assurance



________________________________________________________________________________
Role of NationFiles
	NationFiles acts as an aggregator and analytical interpreter, not as a primary intelligence service.



________________________________________________________________________________
Source hygiene
	The basis is event feeds, economic data, demographic metrics, and global open-data initiatives. Sources subject to proven, massive state censorship are downweighted in Layer 2 via trust factors (trust penalty).



________________________________________________________________________________
Error correction process
	When faulty raw data are identified: report to data@nationfiles.com → source review → correction of the faulty data point in the database → automatic score recalculation. Manual overwriting of the final score is prohibited. System-relevant corrections are documented.



________________________________________________________________________________
V. Interpretation limits & forecast disclaimer



________________________________________________________________________________
Forecasts (predictive layer)
	Forecast values are solely statistical extrapolations of current volatilities (including Weighted Least Squares, mean reversion). They visualize the mathematical inertia of a system, not certainties.



________________________________________________________________________________
Limitations
	NationFiles does not model irrational human decisions. Unexpected events (e.g. coup, natural disaster) cannot be predicted from historical data. Scores assess a country's resilience to such shocks, not the occurrence of the shock itself.



________________________________________________________________________________
VI. Data protection & security



________________________________________________________________________________
Privacy by design
	NationFiles does not store personal data of anonymous website visitors. No user profiles are created for political orientation or sold to third parties.



________________________________________________________________________________
Security
	Databases, API interfaces, and the Naciro Core Engine are protected by modern encryption (in-transit and at-rest) and regular penetration testing.

________________________________________________________________________________
