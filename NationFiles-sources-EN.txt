________________________________________________________________________________
Methodology
	The data sources listed on this page are connected to the Naciro Intelligence Engine via the DataSourceConnector system. Fetching runs on a schedule (Cron) according to the intervals shown in the table. After import, raw data is filtered, cleaned and aggregated, and feeds into visualizations (e.g. maps, dashboards) as well as stability and prediction models. Each source may have a weight (score); values are shown in the Connector column of the registry. The methodology complies with the transparency requirements of this source registry.
