# NationFiles & Naciro Engine - Technical Research Archive

This repository contains the official technical documentation, research papers, and validation reports for the **NationFiles** platform and the **Naciro AI Engine**. 

NationFiles is a global, real-time intelligence simulation designed for geopolitical risk assessment, powered by the deterministic Naciro inference engine.

## 📂 Research Directory Overview

All detailed publications are located in the `/research/` folder. This archive follows a cumulative logic, ranging from strategic overviews to deep-level hardware architecture notes and scientific validation.

### Core Documentation

1. **[Validation and Verification Report (VVR)](./nationfiles-stability-index--nfsi--validation-and-verification-report.pdf)** The definitive scientific methodology report regarding the integrity, data provenance, and auditable traceability of the NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI).

2. **[The Large Processing Unit (LPU) Architecture](./the_large_processing_unit__lpu__architecture.pdf)** Technical analysis of the hardware backbone. Focuses on the Tensor Streaming Processor (TSP) paradigm and SRAM-dominant inference to overcome the von Neumann bottleneck.

3. **[The Naciro Engine: A Technical Manual](./The_Naciro_Engine__A_Technical_Manual.pdf)** A comprehensive deep-dive into the autonomous AI system, detailing deterministic neural paths and high-throughput inference protocols.

4. **[The Global Re-Evaluation Framework (GRF)](./The_Global_Re-Evaluation_Framework.pdf)** Methodology of the 24-hour world re-interpretation cycle used to neutralize recency bias in geopolitical modeling.

### Geopolitical & Strategic Research

5. **[Real-time Geopolitical Stability Modeling (Technical Study)](./Real-time_Geopolitical_Stability_Modeling.pdf)** Investigation of high-frequency data ingestion and causality simulations (Forex-Geopolitical Nexus).

6. **[Real-time Geopolitical Stability Modeling (Framework)](./NationFiles_-_Real-time_Geopolitical_Stability_Modeling.pdf)** Academic foundation for the dynamic, 24/7 simulation of global stability indicators.

7. **[The Role of Predictive Layers in Modern Diplomacy](./NationFiles_-_The_Role_of_Predictive_Layers_in_Modern_Diplomacy.pdf)** Analysis of AI-driven foresight as a tool for crisis prevention in international relations.

8. **[Implementing Global Intelligence Systems](./Implementing_Global_Intelligence_Systems.pdf)** Documentation of the Layer 1-3 infrastructure for autonomous signal harvesting and predictive output.

9. **[Predictive Intelligence for Strategic Decisions](./Predictive_Intelligence_for_Strategic_Decisions.pdf)** Strategic guide for integrating the NFSI barometer into corporate and governmental risk management.

10. **[AI-Driven OSINT Fusion - Next-Gen Intelligence](./NationFiles_-_AI-Driven_OSINT_Fusion_-_Next-Gen_Intelligence.pdf)** Technical exploration of autonomous Open Source Intelligence fusion within LPU environments.
