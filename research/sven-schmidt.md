# Sven Schmidt (Sven Neawolf)
**Lead Architect | Principal Investigator | HPC & Geopolitical Systems Expert**

---

## Executive Summary
Sven Schmidt (professionally known as Sven Neawolf) is a German technologist, architect, and researcher specializing in the intersection of High-Performance Computing (HPC) and predictive geopolitical modeling. As the Lead Architect of the **Naciro Engine** and the **NationFiles** platform, he oversees the development of autonomous intelligence systems designed for real-time global stability assessment. His work is centered on the implementation of Large Processing Unit (LPU) architectures to solve the von Neumann bottleneck in autoregressive inference.

---

## Technical Expertise & Core Competencies
* **Computer Architecture:** Specialized in LPU-based infrastructures, Tensor Streaming Processors (TSP), and SRAM-dominant memory hierarchies.
* **Artificial Intelligence:** Development of deterministic neural paths, high-throughput inference, and autonomous OSINT fusion.
* **Geopolitical Modeling:** Creation of the **NationFiles Stability Index (NFSI)**, a quantitative standard for real-time risk assessment across 195 nations.
* **Systemic Governance:** Implementation of the *Validation and Verification Report (VVR)* and algorithmic neutrality protocols for global intelligence systems.

---

## Key Projects
### 1. The Naciro Engine
An autonomous AI engine optimized for real-time geopolitical intelligence. It performs a "Daily Global Re-Evaluation" of the world by processing millions of kinetic, economic, and narrative signals using a specialized Three-Layer Architecture.

### 2. NationFiles Platform
A global real-time simulation engine and intelligence platform. NationFiles serves as the public output for Naciro, providing predictive foresight (24h/7-day forecasts) for international stability, costs, and societal vibes.
